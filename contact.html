FAQ

Common Questions

What languages do you train in?

I offer workshops in both English and German. Code examples are typically language-agnostic or in C#/Java/TypeScript, but we can adapt to your team's stack.

How many people can attend a workshop?

For optimal interaction, I recommend 8-16 participants for hands-on workshops. Larger groups can work for talks and presentations. Let's discuss your specific needs.

Do you offer remote workshops?

Yes! I've refined my remote workshop format over years of experience. Using collaborative tools like Miro, we achieve the same interactive experience as in-person.

Can workshops be customized?

Absolutely. For in-house workshops, I can tailor the content to your specific domain, technology stack, and learning objectives. We'll discuss this during planning.
